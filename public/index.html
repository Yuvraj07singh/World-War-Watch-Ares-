What Midnight Means
Midnight on the Doomsday Clock does not mean a specific event. It represents the point at which global catastrophe — nuclear exchange, civilizational collapse, irreversible climate breakdown — becomes unavoidable. The clock is maintained by the Bulletin of Atomic Scientists, including Nobel laureates. At 89 seconds, they are saying: humanity has never been closer to self-destruction than right now.
